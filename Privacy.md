# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 6 March, 2026

---

## Lunary Privacy Center

Welcome to **Lunary** ("we", "us", or "our").

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it while providing a high-quality Discord music experience. We only collect the minimum amount of data necessary for the bot to function.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 User Data
| Data Type | Purpose | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Discord User ID** | Identification & settings | Unique identifier used to link your preferences. |
| **Music Preferences** | Personalization | Saved volume settings or custom filters (e.g., Bassboost, Nightcore). |
| **Favorites/Playlists** | Storage | Only tracks or playlists you explicitly save via commands. |
| **Usage Statistics** | Improvement | Total commands used to optimize bot performance. |

### 1.2 Server (Guild) Data
| Data Type | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Guild ID** | To save server-specific configurations. |
| **Settings** | Prefix, 24/7 mode status, and designated music channels. |
| **Active Queues** | To maintain music playback and state during your session. |

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used strictly for bot functionality and performance.

### Core Features
* **Playback**: Streaming music in voice channels with low latency.
* **Queue Management**: Keeping track of your songs and autoplay preferences.
* **24/7 Mode**: Remembering which voice channels to remain in.

### Personalization
* Applying your saved audio filters (8D, Lofi, etc.) to your stream.
* Remembering your custom prefix and server-specific settings.

---

## 3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. All information remains internal and is used only for operating Lunary.

---

## 4. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including secure database storage (MongoDB/Redis) and restricted access to bot configuration files.

---

## 5. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your data.
* **Server Data**: Kicking the bot from your server will result in the eventual cleanup of guild settings.
* **User Data**: You may contact us via our support server to request a manual wipe of your stored preferences.
